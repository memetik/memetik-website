# XoomAI - Loom Script

## Working Title
Why XoomAI should own unbranded decision-stage research before buyers build a shortlist without them

## Opener
XoomAI does not have a category-demand problem. It has a visibility problem.

There are 14,845,730 validated searches in this category, and 97.6% of that opportunity is unbranded.

Right now, XoomAI has 0 organic traffic, 0 organic keywords, and a sampled AI visibility rate of 0 out of 16 across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview.

## Breakdown
That means buyers can research AI workflow automation and managed AI employee options without ever encountering XoomAI.

The real issue is not just missed traffic. It is missed inclusion in the research moments where shortlists get built.

The highest-priority opening wedge is buyer-guide and evaluation queries such as AI tools, best AI, best AI for coding, AI workflow automation tools, and AI workflow tools.

From a traffic standpoint, that is where discoverability can start. From a revenue standpoint, that is where commercial visibility begins. From an AI visibility standpoint, that is the content layer answer engines can reference.

The plan models 114,910 visits in the first 6 months and 310,568 in 12 months, but those are modeled outcomes, not guarantees.

Revenue planning still requires XoomAI's own ACV, AOV, and funnel inputs before anyone turns traffic planning into a revenue model.

## First Move
Start with buyer-guide pages around core evaluation topics.

Add comparison pages, use-case pages, and pricing-support pages that help buyers choose.

Support those pages with proof signals through reviews, community participation, listicles, editorial mentions, and backlinks.

Use the same thesis across every asset: XoomAI should show up before buyers commit to other options.

Measure progress first through discoverability and shortlist inclusion, not through invented revenue certainty.

## Close
The opportunity is real, but the current visibility gap is bigger than most founders assume.

If you want the first buyer-guide, comparison, and proof pages I'd ship for XoomAI, comment visibility.

I'll break down the opening wedge and the sequence behind it.
