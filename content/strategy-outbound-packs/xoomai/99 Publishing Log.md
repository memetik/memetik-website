## Recommended Sequence
- Start with the traffic angle as the top-of-funnel thesis: large category demand, 97.6% unbranded, 0 current visibility.
- Follow with the revenue angle to reframe the conversation from forecast-first to shortlist-visibility-first.
- Then publish the AI visibility angle to show why search alone is no longer enough.
- Repurpose the strongest hooks into short videos and a carousel for each angle.
- Use the Loom after the first wave of posts to tie the three angles into one campaign story.
- Run comment-to-DM follow-up on every post using the same CTA keyword: visibility.
- Use cold email after public content is live so outreach can point back to the same thesis and assets.

- Keep one thesis across every asset: XoomAI should own unbranded, decision-stage research before buyers build a shortlist without them.
