# XoomAI - AI Visibility Angle

## Core Angle
XoomAI is absent from the AI-assisted answers that help buyers build shortlists, so the brand is losing mindshare before a sales conversation even starts.

## Why It Matters Now
Sampled prompts already show the market being explained by generic tools and better-known platforms. If XoomAI does not publish selection content and support it with third-party proof, AI-assisted research will keep routing buyers elsewhere.

## Approved Proof Points
- Sampled AI visibility: 0/16 mention rate across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview.
- In sampled answers for best managed AI employee and workflow automation, XoomAI was not mentioned.
- Sampled answers referenced tools and platforms such as Zapier, UiPath, Lindy, Devin, GPT-based agents, Perplexity, and Claude instead.
- Commercial objective: move from discoverability to shortlist inclusion across Google, ChatGPT, Gemini, and other answer surfaces.
- Core pages should be reinforced with review-platform work, community participation, listicles, editorial mentions, and backlinks.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not claim visibility data for platforms that were not successfully validated.
- Do not generalize sampled results into universal platform certainty.
- Do not say AI visibility can be won with on-site pages alone.
- Do not claim XoomAI is already recommended by answer engines.
