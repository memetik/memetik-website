# XoomAI - Carousel Outline

## Traffic

### Slide 01 — XoomAI does not have a demand problem
The category already shows 14,845,730 validated searches. The gap is visibility, not market size.

### Slide 02 — Most of the opportunity is unbranded
14,494,820 searches, or 97.6% of the opportunity, sit outside competitor brand terms. Buyers are researching the market in the open.

### Slide 03 — XoomAI is not capturing that demand yet
Current organic traffic is 0. Current organic keywords are 0. Buyers can research the category without ever seeing XoomAI.

### Slide 04 — The first move is decision-stage content
Start with buyer guides, comparison pages, use-case pages, and support content around terms like AI tools, best AI, best AI for coding, AI workflow automation tools, and AI workflow tools.

### Slide 05 — Model the upside, do not promise it
The modeled target is 114,910 visits in the first 6 months and 310,568 visits in 12 months. These are planning targets, not guarantees.

### Slide 06 — Win before the shortlist is formed
XoomAI should own unbranded, decision-stage research before buyers build a shortlist without them. Comment visibility if you want the first page sequence.

## Revenue

### Slide 01 — Revenue planning starts with visibility
If buyers cannot find XoomAI during evaluation, any revenue forecast is built on missing inputs.

### Slide 02 — The current commercial footprint is thin
XoomAI has 0 organic traffic, 0 organic keywords, and no ranked commercial keywords found.

### Slide 03 — Commercial intent already exists
The market includes buyer-guide demand and pricing-related demand, including terms such as AI assistant pricing and AI agent pricing.

### Slide 04 — The first commercial wedge is buyer guides
Show up where buyers compare options first. Then support that with use-case, comparison, and pricing-support pages.

### Slide 05 — Traffic models are not revenue forecasts
The plan models 114,910 visits in 6 months and 310,568 in 12 months, but revenue planning still requires XoomAI's own ACV, AOV, and funnel inputs.

### Slide 06 — Fix shortlist absence before forecasting pipeline
Get XoomAI into decision-stage research first. Then layer first-party commercial inputs onto the visibility plan. Comment visibility for the page sequence.

## AI Visibility

### Slide 01 — Search is not the only visibility layer now
Buyers increasingly use AI-assisted answers to learn who the best options are before they ever visit a website.

### Slide 02 — XoomAI is absent from sampled AI answers
Across sampled prompts in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview, XoomAI's mention rate was 0 out of 16.

### Slide 03 — Other tools and platforms are being surfaced first
Sampled answers referenced tools and platforms such as Zapier, UiPath, Lindy, Devin, GPT-based agents, Perplexity, and Claude instead.

### Slide 04 — That creates a shortlist problem
If buyers build their first shortlist inside AI-assisted research, XoomAI loses mindshare before a sales conversation starts.

### Slide 05 — The fix is pages plus proof
Publish selection content, then reinforce it with review-platform work, community participation, listicles, editorial mentions, and backlinks.

### Slide 06 — The goal is shortlist inclusion
XoomAI should become visible across search and AI answer surfaces before buyers lock in other options. Comment visibility for the first wedge.
