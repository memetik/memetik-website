# XoomAI - Revenue Angle

## Core Angle
XoomAI's revenue conversation should start with commercial visibility, not made-up forecasts. If buyers cannot find XoomAI during evaluation, revenue modeling is guesswork.

## Why It Matters Now
The category already contains decision-stage and pricing-related demand, but XoomAI has no current organic footprint and no ranked commercial keywords. The first commercial job is to get into shortlist-forming queries, then layer in first-party ACV/AOV and funnel data for revenue planning.

## Approved Proof Points
- Buyer Guides is the highest-priority commercial wedge.
- No ranked commercial keywords found.
- Pricing & Cost demand exists in the market, including terms such as AI assistant pricing and AI agent pricing.
- Modeled first 6-month target: 114,910 visits.
- Modeled 12-month base case: 310,568 visits.
- Revenue planning requires XoomAI's own ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not give a revenue forecast or ROI promise.
- Do not imply conversion rates, close rates, or payback without XoomAI's first-party data.
- Do not present modeled traffic as booked pipeline.
- Do not pretend pricing-page demand alone will drive the strategy.
