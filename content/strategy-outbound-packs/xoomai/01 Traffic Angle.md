# XoomAI - Traffic Angle

## Core Angle
XoomAI's biggest traffic miss is not competitor-brand demand. It is the open market of unbranded, decision-stage searches where buyers are already choosing tools, providers, and approaches.

## Why It Matters Now
XoomAI is starting from zero organic visibility in a market where nearly all reachable demand is unbranded. That makes buyer-guide and evaluation pages the fastest way to earn discoverability instead of waiting for brand demand to form.

## Approved Proof Points
- Total validated search opportunity: 14,845,730.
- Unbranded / non-competitor demand: 14,494,820, or 97.6% of the market.
- Current organic traffic: 0.
- Current organic keywords: 0.
- First pages to ship: AI tools, best AI, best AI for coding, AI workflow automation tools, and AI workflow tools.
- Modeled first 6-month target: 114,910 visits.
- Modeled 12-month base case: 310,568 visits.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not say broad AI awareness is the first move.
- Do not promise the modeled traffic outcomes as guaranteed.
- Do not say XoomAI already owns category visibility.
- Do not invent ranking positions, click-through rates, or competitor losses.
