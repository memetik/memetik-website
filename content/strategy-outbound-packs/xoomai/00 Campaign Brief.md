# XoomAI - Campaign Brief

## Core Thesis
XoomAI should win growth by owning unbranded, decision-stage research for AI workflow automation and managed AI employee selection before buyers build a shortlist without them.

## Why This Pack Exists
The category is large, mostly unbranded, and already being shaped by search and AI-assisted research. With 97.6% of demand outside competitor brand terms, zero current organic visibility, and zero sampled mentions across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview, XoomAI risks being left out while buyers choose tools, platforms, and providers now.

## Approved Proof Points
- Validated topical demand across the category: 14,845,730 searches.
- Modeled first 6-month target: 114,910 visits.
- Modeled 12-month base case: 310,568 visits.
- Aggressive modeled upside: 538,574 visits.
- Unbranded / non-competitor demand: 14,494,820 searches, or 97.6% of the opportunity.
- Current organic traffic: 0.
- Current organic keywords: 0.
- Sampled AI visibility: 0/16 mention rate across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview.
- Highest-priority opening wedge: buyer-guide and evaluation queries such as AI tools, best AI, best AI for coding, AI workflow automation tools, and AI workflow tools.
- Revenue planning requires XoomAI's own ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not claim guaranteed traffic, pipeline, or revenue.
- Do not say XoomAI already has answer-engine momentum or established authority momentum.
- Do not imply visibility across platforms that were not validated in the brief.
- Do not invent competitor comparisons, case studies, win rates, or conversion rates.
- Do not present modeled upside as certainty.
- Do not give revenue forecasts without first-party ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.

## Primary CTA
- Type: comment
- CTA: visibility
- Follow-up offer: I'll break down the first buyer-guide, comparison, and proof pages I'd ship for XoomAI.

## Angle Map
### Traffic
XoomAI's biggest traffic miss is not competitor-brand demand. It is the open market of unbranded, decision-stage searches where buyers are already choosing tools, providers, and approaches.

### Revenue
XoomAI's revenue conversation should start with commercial visibility, not made-up forecasts. If buyers cannot find XoomAI during evaluation, revenue modeling is guesswork.

### AI Visibility
XoomAI is absent from the AI-assisted answers that help buyers build shortlists, so the brand is losing mindshare before a sales conversation even starts.
