# Hyro - Campaign Brief

## Core Thesis
Hyro's fastest growth move is to own the decision-stage electrolyte drink mix queries where buyers choose a product, then reinforce those pages with third-party proof so the brand becomes easier to find, compare, and mention before shoppers default to incumbents.

## Why This Pack Exists
Hyro already has a usable authority base with 74 referring domains, but only 1,004 organic visits and 362 ranking keywords in a category with 8,464,050 validated searches. The biggest near-term wedge is open now: buyer-guide demand is clear, 92.6% of the validated opportunity is non-competitor and unbranded, and Hyro was absent in sampled commercial prompts across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview.

## Approved Proof Points
- Hyro currently has 1,004 organic visits and 362 organic keywords.
- The validated search opportunity is 8,464,050.
- Modeled, estimate-only traffic potential is 70,857 in the first 6 months and 191,505 in 12 months, with aggressive upside of 327,134.
- Buyer Guides demand is 211,170 and includes terms like best electrolyte drink, best electrolyte powder, best hydration powder, best electrolyte mix, and best electrolyte drink mix.
- 92.6% of the validated opportunity is non-competitor and unbranded.
- Hyro has 74 referring domains and 366 backlinks, which is enough authority to support a focused wedge.
- In sampled commercial prompts, Hyro had 0/8 mentions across ChatGPT, 0/8 across Gemini, and 0/8 across Google AI Overview.
- Competitors currently shaping the category visibility layer include LMNT, Liquid I.V., Nuun, and DripDrop.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not promise guaranteed traffic, rankings, or business results.
- Do not say Hyro is invisible across all search or all AI; only say it was absent in sampled commercial prompts.
- Do not invent revenue certainty or ROI; revenue planning requires first-party ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.
- Do not frame the opportunity as only competitor capture when most of the validated opportunity is unbranded.
- Do not use unsupported platform certainty beyond the sampled prompt data.

## Angle Map
### Traffic
Hyro is not losing because the electrolyte market is closed. It is losing the exact decision-stage queries where buyers choose which brand to trust.

### Revenue
Hyro's commercial gap is shortlist access. The brand needs to show up where buyers compare options, not just where people browse the category.

### AI Visibility
Hyro is missing the answer surfaces that increasingly influence who gets mentioned when buyers ask for the best electrolyte option.
