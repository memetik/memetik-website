# Hyro - Traffic Angle

## Core Angle
Hyro is not losing because the electrolyte market is closed. It is losing the exact decision-stage queries where buyers choose which brand to trust.

## Why It Matters Now
The fastest path is to own buyer-guide and comparison pages first, while the open-market demand is still mostly unbranded and before broader category expansion.

## Approved Proof Points
- Hyro has 1,004 organic visits today versus a modeled first 6-month target of 70,857 and a modeled 12-month path of 191,505.
- Buyer-guide demand alone is 211,170.
- The first pages to ship are best electrolyte drink, best electrolyte powder, best hydration powder, best electrolyte mix, and best electrolyte drink mix.
- 92.6% of the validated opportunity is non-competitor and unbranded.
- Hyro already has 74 referring domains, which is enough authority to support a focused opening move.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not present modeled traffic as guaranteed.
- Do not say Hyro should try to rank for every electrolyte term at once.
- Do not position broad awareness content as the first priority.
- Do not ignore the need for supporting proof and clean publishing basics.
