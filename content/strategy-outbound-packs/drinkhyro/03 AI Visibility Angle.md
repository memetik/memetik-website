# Hyro - AI Visibility Angle

## Core Angle
Hyro is missing the answer surfaces that increasingly influence who gets mentioned when buyers ask for the best electrolyte option.

## Why It Matters Now
In sampled commercial prompts, Hyro was absent while incumbents were named. That means Hyro needs pages that are easier to cite plus stronger third-party proof around those pages.

## Approved Proof Points
- Sampled prompt baseline: 0/8 mentions in ChatGPT, 0/8 in Gemini, and 0/8 in Google AI Overview.
- Prompt evidence shows incumbents like LMNT, Liquid I.V., Nuun, and DripDrop appearing in the category conversation.
- The commercial objective is shortlist inclusion across search and answer surfaces, not just standard rankings.
- Each decision page should be paired with third-party proof, review reinforcement, and clean publishing basics.
- Use caveat discipline: sampled prompt data is usable, but unsupported platform certainty should be avoided.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not say Hyro is absent from every AI system or every prompt.
- Do not claim platform coverage beyond the sampled data.
- Do not suggest on-site pages alone will solve answer-surface visibility.
- Do not promise mentions on a fixed timeline.
