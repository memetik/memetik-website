# Kinso - AI Visibility Angle

## Core Angle
Kinso is not showing up in sampled answer-engine checks for the buying prompts that increasingly shape software selection.

## Why It Matters Now
Search is no longer the only place buyers form opinions. They also ask ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews for recommendations and comparisons. If Kinso is absent there while incumbents frame the category, the market learns about alternatives without Kinso in the set.

## Approved Proof Points
- Sampled visibility is absent across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.
- The prompt evidence for "best shared inbox and email triage software" showed incumbents such as Front and Help Scout appearing instead.
- Kinso's commercial objective in the approved brief is to move from brand-adjacent discoverability to shortlist inclusion across Google, ChatGPT, Gemini, and other answer surfaces.
- Kinso already has a usable authority base with 64 referring domains, but answer-surface consistency is still weak.
- The approved strategy pairs owned decision pages with reviews, community participation, listicles, editorial mentions, and backlinks so Kinso is easier to retrieve and trust.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not claim complete AI visibility coverage beyond the sampled platforms and prompts.
- Do not cite Perplexity or unsupported mention-share figures.
- Do not say Kinso is invisible everywhere online; keep the claim to the sampled answer-surface checks.
- Do not promise immediate AI-answer inclusion from publishing alone.
