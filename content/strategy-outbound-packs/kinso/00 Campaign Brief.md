# Kinso - Campaign Brief

## Core Thesis
Kinso does not need broader awareness first. It needs to become visible in the decision-stage shared inbox and email triage conversations that create shortlist inclusion across search and AI answer surfaces.

## Why This Pack Exists
Kinso has 599 organic visits, 22 ranking keywords, and 64 referring domains in a category with 8,250,150 validated searches. Most of the modeled opportunity is still non-competitor and unbranded, but sampled visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews is absent while larger incumbents already hold more search footprint. The opening still exists, but Kinso needs to own buyer-guide and comparison moments before the category narrative gets set by others.

## Approved Proof Points
- Kinso currently has 599 organic visits and 22 ranking keywords.
- Validated search opportunity totals 8,250,150 across the approved category frame.
- Kinso has 64 referring domains and 435 backlinks, giving it a usable authority base for a focused wedge.
- 94.3% of the modeled demand mix is non-competitor / unbranded, while 5.7% is competitor-keyword demand.
- Sampled visibility is absent across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.
- Buyer Guides are the largest priority cluster, with 4,537,700 in demand and 499,147 modeled 12-month traffic in the base case.
- Modeled upside is estimate-only: 213,103 first 6-month target, 575,954 expected traffic in 12 months, and 859,549 aggressive upside.
- Competitors with materially larger search footprints include Front, Help Scout, Missive, Gmelius, and Drag.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not promise revenue outcomes, ROI, or pipeline certainty.
- Do not present modeled traffic as guaranteed results.
- Do not claim platform visibility beyond the sampled ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overview checks.
- Do not cite Perplexity coverage or unsupported LLM-mention data because those probes were unresolved upstream.
- Do not invent competitor comparisons, feature claims, or category leadership claims not present in the approved brief and strategy page.
- If revenue planning comes up, state plainly that it requires Kinso's ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.
