# Kinso - Traffic Angle

## Core Angle
Kinso is missing the unbranded, decision-stage search demand that shapes shortlist inclusion in shared inbox and email triage software.

## Why It Matters Now
This is not a closed market where every important query is already competitor-owned. Most of the modeled opportunity is still unbranded, which means Kinso can win by owning buyer-guide, use-case, and comparison pages now instead of chasing broader awareness first.

## Approved Proof Points
- Kinso is at 599 organic visits and 22 ranking keywords today.
- Total validated opportunity is 8,250,150.
- 94.3% of the modeled demand mix is non-competitor / unbranded.
- Buyer Guides are the largest priority cluster with 4,537,700 in demand.
- The base model points to 213,103 visits in the first 6 months and 575,954 in 12 months if Kinso wins decision-stage pages first.
- Kinso already has 64 referring domains, which is enough to support a focused content and authority push.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not say Kinso will definitely hit the modeled traffic numbers.
- Do not imply the full 8,250,150 opportunity is immediately capturable.
- Do not describe the market as competitor-free; describe it as largely unbranded.
- Do not drift into revenue claims without first-party ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.
