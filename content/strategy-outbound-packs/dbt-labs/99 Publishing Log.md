## Channels To Prioritize
- LinkedIn (primary — founder-advisory audience, B2B data/dev-tools leaders).
- Twitter/X (secondary — thread versions of each angle, stat graphics).
- Instagram Reels / TikTok (tertiary — short video hooks from each angle).
- Loom (warm outbound — full breakdown for engaged prospects and DM conversations).
- Email (cold outbound — 2-3 batch sends with follow-up and breakup sequences).
- Community engagement (Slack groups, Reddit r/dataengineering, dbt Community Slack — use hooks and stat lines for organic comments, not self-promotion posts).

## Operator Notes
- Lead with the AI Visibility angle — the 0% ChatGPT stat is the most novel and shareable data point. It earns attention before the Traffic and Revenue angles require more context.
- Traffic angle is the workhorse — broadest relevance, easiest for non-data-category founders to map onto their own situation.
- Revenue angle is the closer — deploy it after Traffic and AI Visibility have established the gap. Revenue framing moves engaged prospects toward conversion.
- All traffic and revenue projections must include 'estimate-only,' 'modeled,' or 'projected' language. Do not present any number as guaranteed.
- Revenue impact statements must include the disclaimer that revenue planning requires first-party ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.
- Do not claim dbt is completely absent from all AI surfaces — it does appear in some Google AI Overviews and one Gemini response.
- Do not invent Perplexity data. Do not claim certainty about all ChatGPT results (one prompt timed out).
- Loom script is designed for warm prospects who have engaged via comments or DMs. Do not mass-distribute cold.
- Comment DM follow-up sequences have three touches max. Do not over-follow. The soft-close is the final message.
- Cold email sequences have three touches: initial, follow-up, breakup. Do not add additional follow-ups beyond the breakup.
- All assets remain additive to the website — do not create runtime or routing dependencies. This pack sits downstream of the canonical strategy page.
