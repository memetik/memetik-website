# dbt Labs - Carousel Outline

## Traffic

### Slide 01 — 11.7M Monthly Searches
The data transformation category generates 11.7 million monthly searches. This is one of the largest demand pools in the modern data stack.

### Slide 02 — dbt Labs Captures ~1%
dbt Labs currently receives approximately 111,508 organic monthly visits — roughly 1% of available category demand. The most recognized tool in the space is capturing almost none of it.

### Slide 03 — 97.7% Unbranded Demand
Nearly all of this demand is unbranded — 11.4M monthly searches from buyers looking for a solution, not a specific vendor. This is addressable, unclaimed territory.

### Slide 04 — 1.9M Buyer-Guide Searches. Zero dbt Pages.
The buyer-guide cluster alone — 'best ETL tools,' 'data transformation tools,' 'extract transform load tools' — represents 1.9M monthly searches. dbt Labs has zero owned pages targeting any of them.

### Slide 05 — 21K Comparison Searches. Also Uncaptured.
Bottom-of-funnel queries like 'ETL vs ELT,' 'dbt vs Matillion,' and 'data lake vs data warehouse' generate 21,190 monthly searches. These are the queries where buyers make final vendor decisions. dbt doesn't own them either.

### Slide 06 — The Authority Is Already There
dbt Labs has 5,714 referring domains and 67,348 backlinks — a stronger authority foundation than every direct competitor except Gartner. The problem isn't domain strength. It's page coverage.

### Slide 07 — Competitors With 1/10th the Authority Are Winning
Coalesce.io has approximately 526 referring domains — roughly 1/10th of dbt's authority. It's showing up in AI answers and capturing buyer-intent queries dbt should dominate. The gap is content, not credibility.

### Slide 08 — Modeled Upside: 419K Monthly Visits (Estimate-Only)
Building decision-stage pages against this demand could drive an estimated 419,200 monthly visits within 12 months (base case). First 6-month target: 155,104 monthly visits on the highest-intent queries.

### Slide 09 — Authority Without Pages = Invisible Strength
dbt Labs has the best product and the strongest authority base. But authority without targeted pages is like having the best product in a store with no signage. Buyers walk right past.

## AI Visibility

### Slide 01 — dbt Labs: 0% ChatGPT Mention Rate
We tested 16 real buyer prompts across ChatGPT. dbt Labs — the most recognized transformation tool in modern data — was mentioned zero times.

### Slide 02 — Gemini: 6%. AI Overviews: 12.5%.
Gemini mentioned dbt once out of 16 prompts — as a source link, not the recommended tool. Google AI Overviews mentioned it twice — but cited competitor listicle pages as the source, not dbt Labs content.

### Slide 03 — ChatGPT Didn't Even Understand the Question
When asked 'best data transformation tools,' ChatGPT returned a list of statistical techniques — normalization, standardization, log transforms. It didn't interpret the query as a tooling question. No vendors were mentioned at all.

### Slide 04 — A Competitor With 1/10th the Authority Gets Cited Instead
Coalesce.io has approximately 526 referring domains. dbt Labs has 5,714. Coalesce is getting cited in AI answers for 'best data transformation tools.' Authority without decision-stage content doesn't translate to AI visibility.

### Slide 05 — 60%+ of B2B Buyers Start in AI Search
The shift is already underway. Over 60% of B2B buyers now begin tool research inside AI-powered search surfaces before visiting vendor websites. The recommendation patterns forming today will shape buying consideration for years.

### Slide 06 — This Isn't a Brand Problem
Everyone in data knows dbt. The problem is that AI models can only recommend tools they can find structured decision-stage content for — buyer guides, comparison pages, evaluation frameworks. dbt Labs hasn't built them.

### Slide 07 — Google AI Overview: Mentioned dbt, Cited Competitors
For 'best data transformation tools,' Google's AI Overview listed dbt alongside Matillion, Fivetran, and Alteryx. But every source citation pointed to competitor listicle pages — Datameer, Astera, and Coalesce. Not dbt Labs.

### Slide 08 — The Fix Is Content Infrastructure
Decision-stage pages targeting the highest-intent buyer queries. Structured comparisons. Evaluation frameworks. Paired with the off-site authority dbt already has. The foundation is there — the pages aren't.

### Slide 09 — The Window Is Narrowing
Competitors are building the content AI models reference. Every month dbt Labs waits, the recommendation patterns solidify around competitors with weaker products but stronger content positioning.
