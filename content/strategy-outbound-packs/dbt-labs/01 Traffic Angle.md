# dbt Labs - Traffic Angle

## Core Angle
dbt Labs sits on 11.7M monthly searches of category demand and captures a fraction of it. 97.7% of that demand is unbranded — buyers looking for a solution, not a vendor. The buyer-guide cluster alone represents 1.9M monthly searches with zero owned dbt Labs pages targeting them. This is the largest unclaimed traffic opportunity in data transformation.

## Why It Matters Now
The data transformation category is actively being contested. Competitors like Coalesce, Matillion, and Integrate.io are publishing the buyer-guide and comparison pages that capture this demand. dbt Labs has the strongest authority foundation of any direct competitor (5,714 referring domains), but authority without targeted pages means the traffic flows elsewhere. The modeled base case shows 419,200 monthly visits within 12 months if the right pages exist — up from the current trajectory of brand-adjacent traffic that doesn't convert buyer intent.

## Approved Proof Points
- 11.7M monthly searches in the data transformation category.
- 1.9M monthly searches in buyer-guide queries alone — 'best ETL tools,' 'data transformation tools,' 'extract transform load tools' — with no owned dbt Labs pages targeting them.
- 97.7% of demand is unbranded (11,439,550 searches/month) — buyers searching for solutions, not vendors.
- 21,190 monthly searches in pure bottom-of-funnel alternatives and comparison queries.
- dbt Labs currently has 111,508 organic monthly visits against an 11.7M opportunity — capturing roughly 1% of available demand.
- 419,200 modeled monthly visits achievable within 12 months (estimate-only, base case). Aggressive upside: 666,904.
- First 6-month target: 155,104 monthly visits focused on highest-intent buyer-guide and comparison queries.
- 5,714 referring domains provide the authority base to rank — the gap is page coverage, not domain strength.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not guarantee traffic numbers — all projections are modeled estimates.
- Do not claim dbt Labs has zero organic traffic; it has 111,508 monthly visits currently.
- Do not state revenue impact — revenue planning requires first-party ACV/AOV and funnel inputs.
- Do not imply competitors have 'locked down' the category — the brief states none have locked it down yet.
