# dbt Labs - AI Visibility Angle

## Core Angle
dbt Labs has a 0% ChatGPT mention rate, a 6% Gemini mention rate, and a 12.5% Google AI Overview mention rate across 16 tested buyer prompts. Meanwhile, Coalesce.io — with roughly one-tenth the referring-domain authority — is getting cited in AI answers for the exact queries dbt should dominate. This isn't a brand problem; it's a content infrastructure problem. AI models can only recommend tools they can find structured decision-stage content for, and dbt Labs hasn't built that content.

## Why It Matters Now
Over 60% of B2B buyers now start tool research inside AI-powered search surfaces before visiting vendor websites. The recommendation patterns forming today on ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews will shape buying consideration for years. dbt Labs has a narrow window to build the content infrastructure that gets it recommended — before competitors with less authority but better content positioning become the default answers in the category.

## Approved Proof Points
- 0% ChatGPT mention rate — dbt was not mentioned in any of 16 tested buyer prompts.
- 6.3% Gemini mention rate — dbt was mentioned once out of 16 prompts. The mention was a source link citation; Coalesce.io was the primary cited source.
- 12.5% Google AI Overview mention rate — dbt appeared in 2 out of 16 prompts, but source citations pointed to third-party listicle pages (Datameer, Astera, Coalesce), not dbt Labs owned content.
- Coalesce.io has approximately 526 referring domains vs dbt's 5,714 — roughly 1/10th the authority — yet gets cited more frequently in AI answers for 'best data transformation tools.'
- When a buyer asked ChatGPT 'best data transformation tools,' it returned a generic data science explanation (normalization, standardization, log transforms) — it didn't even interpret the query as a tooling recommendation. dbt wasn't mentioned. No vendor was.
- Gemini produced a detailed ETL vs ELT comparison for 'data transformation comparison' and cited getdbt.com once as a source — but Coalesce.io was the primary source citation.
- Google AI Overview for 'best data transformation tools' listed dbt alongside Matillion, Fivetran, and Alteryx — but every source citation pointed to competitor listicle pages, not to dbt Labs content.
- Over 60% of B2B buyers now start tool research inside AI-powered search surfaces.

## Claims To Avoid
- Do not claim dbt is completely absent from all AI surfaces — it appears in some Google AI Overviews and one Gemini response.
- Do not invent Perplexity data — that platform probe failed and returned no usable results.
- Do not claim certainty about all ChatGPT results — one prompt timed out with no response.
- Do not state a specific percentage for 'buyers using AI search' unless sourced — the 60% figure is a directional industry estimate used in the approved brief.
- Do not imply AI visibility can be 'fixed' overnight — building recommendation share requires sustained content and authority work.
- Do not conflate mention with recommendation — being mentioned as a source link is different from being the recommended tool.
