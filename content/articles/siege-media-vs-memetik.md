## Buying Guide: How to Evaluate Your AEO vs. SEO Needs

### Step 1: Assess Your AI Visibility Gap

Run this diagnostic before talking to any agency:

**The ChatGPT Test**
1. Open ChatGPT and ask: "What are the best [your category] solutions for [your target persona]?"
2. Does your brand appear in the response?
3. If yes, where? (First mention, comparison mention, or alternative mention?)
4. If no, which competitors appear?

**The Perplexity Test**
1. Query Perplexity with your core buyer questions
2. Check citation sources—does your content appear?
3. Note whether competitors dominate AI-generated recommendations

**The Claude Test**
1. Ask Claude about solutions in your category
2. Evaluate whether your brand gets mentioned
3. Assess the characterization if you do appear

**Interpret Your Results:**

- **0-2 platforms cite you**: Critical AI visibility gap requiring immediate AEO
- **3-5 platforms cite you**: Moderate visibility; AEO would improve market position
- **6+ platforms cite you**: Strong foundation; AEO optimization could dominate category
- **Competitors appear but you don't**: Severe competitive disadvantage in AI search
