## Your 90-Day Implementation Roadmap

Let's break down exactly how to go from 0% citation rate to consistent AI visibility in three months, with realistic budgets that work for businesses like Fred's.

### Phase 1: Quick Wins (Days 1-30)

**Budget: $5,000**

Your first month focuses on high-impact, fast-execution optimizations that can generate initial citations within 45-60 days.

**FAQ Schema Implementation ($2,000):** Create 20-30 comprehensive FAQ pages answering specific questions prospects ask in your domain. Each FAQ needs 200-400 words providing genuinely helpful answers, structured with proper FAQPage schema markup. These pages become citation magnets because LLMs prioritize content explicitly formatted as question-answer pairs.

**Structured Data Markup on Existing Content ($1,500):** Audit your 15 best-performing pages and implement Article schema with proper entity markup. Add author information, publication dates, and content structure signals that help LLMs understand context and authority.

**AEO-Optimized Pillar Pages ($1,500):** Develop 3-5 comprehensive pillar pages (2,500+ words each) covering your core topics with depth LLMs consider citation-worthy. Each page needs internal linking structure, clear hierarchy, and factual information backed by data.

**Time commitment:** 10 hours weekly from your team for oversight, coordination, and approval. The heavy lifting—writing, schema implementation, technical setup—gets outsourced to specialists who understand AEO requirements.

Initial citations typically appear days 45-60 as LLMs encounter and process your newly structured content. Don't expect massive visibility yet—quick wins prove the methodology works and build momentum for phase two.

### Phase 2: Infrastructure Building (Days 31-60)

**Budget: $7,000**

Month two focuses on systematic content deployment and establishing measurement systems.

**Programmatic Cluster Pages ($4,000):** Deploy 30 cluster pages using programmatic templates. Each supports your pillar content with specific subtopics, use cases, or comparison angles. The key is maintaining quality while achieving volume—1,200-1,800 words per page with proper structure and internal linking.

**Citation Tracking Setup ($2,000):** Implement systematic LLM monitoring across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude for 30-50 category-relevant queries. This creates your baseline measurement and identifies which content types generate citations most effectively.

**Entity Optimization and Brand Consistency Audit ($1,000):** Ensure your brand entity appears consistently across your website, social profiles, and third-party mentions. **Time commitment:** 10-12 hours weekly as you review citation data and adjust content strategy based on early results.

By day 60, you should see measurable improvement in citation rates—typically 8-15% for focused category queries as your content infrastructure reaches critical mass.
