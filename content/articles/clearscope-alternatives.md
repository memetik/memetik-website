For teams creating **20-100 articles monthly**, mid-tier platforms like Surfer SEO ($89/month) or Clearscope ($189/month) offer the sophistication needed without enterprise complexity. You'll benefit from real-time optimization and workflow integrations with your CMS.

Teams publishing **100-500+ articles monthly** need enterprise platforms with programmatic SEO capabilities.
