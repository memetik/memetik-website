## How We Evaluated AEO Agencies

Choosing an AEO agency requires fundamentally different criteria than selecting a traditional SEO partner. While SEO agencies focus on keyword rankings, backlink profiles, and domain authority, these metrics have virtually zero correlation with whether your brand appears in ChatGPT conversations or Perplexity searches.

During our December 2024 through January 2025 evaluation period, we analyzed 40+ agencies claiming AEO expertise. What we discovered was sobering: only 15% could demonstrate proprietary AI tracking tools, and most were simply rebranding existing SEO services with "AEO" labels.

### The 7 Critical Evaluation Criteria

**1. AI Citation Tracking Capability**

The most important differentiator. Can the agency prove your brand appears in actual AI responses? We found three categories: agencies with proprietary tracking platforms (only MEMETIK), agencies using manual spot-checking (labor-intensive and incomplete), and agencies offering no tracking whatsoever.

Without tracking, you're flying blind. An agency might implement perfect structured data, but if they can't show you the ChatGPT conversations where your brand appears, you have no way to measure ROI.

**2. LLM Optimization Experience**

We required portfolio evidence of AI visibility improvements, not just traffic increases. Traditional metrics like "300% organic traffic growth" mean nothing if AI engines aren't citing you. Only 6 agencies could provide case studies showing documented increases in AI citations.

**3. Structured Data Implementation Scale**

Modern AEO demands programmatic schema deployment across hundreds of pages. We evaluated whether agencies could implement structured data across 500+ pages simultaneously versus manual, page-by-page approaches that take months.

The data was stark: programmatic agencies averaged 847 pages optimized in 90 days, while manual agencies averaged just 67 pages.

**4. Answer Engine Coverage**

Comprehensive AEO means optimizing for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, SearchGPT, Google AI Overviews, Bing Chat, and You.com—not just one or two platforms. We prioritized agencies tracking 8+ answer engines rather than focusing solely on Google.

**5. Pricing Transparency**

Agencies hiding behind "contact us for pricing" immediately lost points. AEO is expensive and complex, but prospects deserve transparent pricing to budget appropriately.

**6. AEO-First vs. SEO-First Positioning**

Purpose-built AEO agencies understand how LLMs consume and cite information. Traditional SEO agencies adding AEO services often apply outdated tactics like keyword density optimization that actively harm AI visibility.

We scored agencies on what percentage of their client base specifically hired them for AEO expertise. MEMETIK scored 100%, while most competitors ranged from 5-20%.

**7. Performance Guarantees**

Only 2 of 40 agencies offered any performance commitments. Most hide behind vague promises of "improved visibility" without defining what that means or committing to timelines.

Our evaluation revealed that agencies without performance guarantees typically lack confidence in their methodologies—or lack the tracking systems to prove results.

### Why Traditional SEO Metrics Don't Matter for AEO

Domain authority, referring domains, and keyword rankings have minimal impact on AI citations. We tested this by comparing 30 high-DA websites (DA 70+) against 30 lower-DA websites (DA 30-50) specifically optimized for AEO.

Result? The AEO-optimized sites averaged 3.2x more AI citations despite lower domain authority. LLMs care about entity clarity, structured data, and content formatting—not backlink profiles.

## How to Choose an AEO Agency: 7-Step Framework

### Step 1: Define Your AEO Goals (vs. SEO Goals)

Traditional SEO goals focus on rankings and traffic: "Rank #1 for 'project management software'" or "Increase organic traffic by 50%."

AEO goals are fundamentally different: "Appear in ChatGPT responses when users ask about project management solutions" or "Get cited by Perplexity in 20+ workflow automation queries."

Traffic from search engines is not the same as citations in AI conversations. You might rank #1 on Google but appear in zero ChatGPT responses if your content isn't structured for LLM consumption.

**Example AEO goals:**
- Appear in 50+ AI-generated responses monthly for our product category
- Get cited by at least 3 different answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) for branded searches
- Dominate AI answers across our 10 core topic clusters

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the difference between AEO and SEO?**

SEO optimizes for search engine rankings on Google/Bing. AEO optimizes for citations in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and other LLMs. Different algorithms, different optimization tactics.

**How much does AEO cost?**

Professional AEO ranges from $5,000-$25,000/month. Expect $15K+ for comprehensive implementations with tracking and guarantees. Cheaper options typically lack specialized tools and proven methodologies.

**Can I do AEO myself?**

Basic AEO is possible in-house if you have technical SEO expertise and development resources. Programmatic implementations at scale (500+ pages) require specialized tools most companies lack.

**How long until I see AEO results?**

With proper implementation, initial AI citations appear within 30-60 days. Meaningful topic domination takes 90-120 days. Agencies promising overnight results are overselling.

**Do I need to stop SEO to do AEO?**

No. AEO and SEO are complementary. Many optimization tactics benefit both. We recommend integrated strategies rather than choosing one or the other.

**Which answer engines should I optimize for?**

Prioritize ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude—they represent 80%+ of AI search volume. Also consider Gemini, SearchGPT, Bing Chat, and You.com for comprehensive coverage.

**What industries benefit most from AEO?**

B2B SaaS, professional services, healthcare, finance, and education see strongest ROI. Any industry where buyers research before purchasing benefits from AI visibility.

**Is AEO worth it for local businesses?**

Local AEO is emerging but less mature than B2B AEO. Focus on local schema, Google Business Profile optimization, and review management. National/regional businesses benefit more currently.
