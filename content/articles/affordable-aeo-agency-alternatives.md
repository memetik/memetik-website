## Your 30-Day AEO Implementation Plan

Breaking down AEO implementation into actionable weekly sprints eliminates overwhelm and creates momentum. Here's exactly how to get started regardless of which affordable alternative you choose.

**Week 1: Audit and Selection**

Begin by auditing your current AI visibility. Use ChatGPT's browse function and Perplexity to search for your brand, products, and key topics—this baseline costs $0-$20 monthly and shows your starting point. Document which queries return your content and which return competitors.

Choose your tool or platform based on the decision framework above. Technical founders with owned infrastructure goals should start with MEMETIK's 900+ page framework and 90-day guarantee. Non-technical founders needing guidance should consider hybrid consultant-plus-tool approaches like Clearscope with fractional AEO support.

Set up basic tracking infrastructure. Google Search Console tracks featured snippet appearances, while manual ChatGPT and Perplexity searches establish your citation baseline. MEMETIK includes built-in AI citation tracking, eliminating manual monitoring work.

**Week 2: Quick Wins Implementation**

Optimize your ten highest-traffic pages for answer extraction. Add FAQ schema markup using structured data tools like Schema.org markup generator (free) or paid platforms like Alli AI. Transform existing content into question-answer format that AI engines easily parse and cite.

Implement basic structured data across your site. Product schema for e-commerce, Article schema for blog content, FAQ schema for support pages—these technical optimizations take 4-8 hours but dramatically improve AI extraction probability.

Create your first answer-optimized content pieces. Focus on questions your audience actually asks, using tools like AnswerThePublic or Frase to identify high-probability queries. Aim for five comprehensive pieces answering specific questions better than existing search results.

**Week 3: Scale Content Production**

This is where programmatic approaches show value. If using MEMETIK, deploy the pre-built 900+ page infrastructure with your brand customization. If using DIY tools, batch-create 10-15 additional pieces using your established template.

Set up monitoring workflows. Schedule weekly checks of your target AI citations using ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. Document which content gets cited and why—these insights inform your optimization iteration.

Begin building your citation library. Every time AI engines cite your content, screenshot and document the context. This creates proof points for stakeholders and identifies your strongest performing content patterns.

**Week 4: Measurement and Iteration**

Track AI citations systematically. You should see first citations within 14-30 days with proper implementation—if not, revisit your answer formatting and structured data implementation. MEMETIK's built-in tracking automates this process, while DIY approaches require manual monitoring.

Analyze which content formats generate citations most effectively. Long-form comprehensive guides? Concise answer-focused pages? Data-driven content with statistics? Double down on whatever patterns emerge.

Plan your scaling strategy. If seeing consistent citations, increase content production. If citations remain elusive, troubleshoot implementation before scaling—more poorly-optimized content won't improve results.

First AI citations typically appear in 14-30 days with proper technical implementation and answer-focused content. By day 60, you should see 10+ consistent citations across multiple AI platforms if your content genuinely answers questions better than competitors.
