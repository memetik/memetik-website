## How the Guaranteed Growth Model Actually Works

Behind every performance guarantee sits concrete measurement infrastructure. We don't rely on third-party tools or manual checking. Our citation tracking system queries AI platforms 3,600+ times every month through a programmatic framework built specifically for AEO measurement.

The foundation is a network of 900+ content pages optimized for buyer-intent queries relevant to your industry. Each week, we use API integrations to ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini variations of these queries. Every response gets captured, parsed, and analyzed in a vector database that identifies brand mentions, contextual positioning, source attribution, and competitive displacement.

We track 12 specific KPIs across these responses:

1. **Citation frequency**: How many times your brand appears in AI answers for target queries
2. **Source attribution percentage**: How often the AI cites your content as the information source
3. **Answer position ranking**: Where your brand appears in multi-option responses (position 1 vs. position 5 matters)
4. **Brand mention sentiment**: Positive, neutral, or negative framing in the AI's language
5. **Competitor displacement**: Instances where you're cited instead of category leaders
6. **Query coverage expansion**: Growth in the breadth of questions triggering citations
7. **Multi-platform consistency**: Citation presence across all four AI engines
8. **Direct vs. indirect mentions**: Named explicitly vs. implied through content citation
9. **Response depth**: Superficial mention vs. detailed explanation featuring your solution
10. **Citation velocity**: Week-over-week growth rate in new citations
11. **Attribution accuracy**: Correct vs. hallucinated details about your offering
12. **Recommendation strength**: "Consider X" vs. "X is the best option for Y scenario"

Your performance bonus gets calculated monthly based on progress across these metrics. We measure the trailing 30-day period, compare it to your baseline audit, and calculate payout accordingly. If we hit 100% of agreed benchmarks, you pay the full performance allocation. If we hit 75%, you pay 75% of that $4,000 (in our $10K example). Fall below 50%, and penalty clauses trigger.

Those penalty clauses are where our model diverges sharply from every competitor. Traditional agencies have no financial downside for underperformance. We deduct $500-$2,000 from our invoice for each primary KPI we miss. On a $10,000 contract with four primary benchmarks, missing all of them costs us $4,000-$8,000 in penalties—we'd effectively pay you to remain a client.

This creates skin in the game. When our compensation depends on your citation growth, we optimize differently than agencies billing hourly or collecting fixed fees. Our team monitors citation performance daily, not quarterly. We pivot strategies within weeks, not after six-month retrospectives.

Clients access this data through our real-time dashboard. You're not waiting for PDF reports emailed monthly. You log in and see this week's ChatGPT citation count compared to last week's. You receive Slack alerts when a new high-value query starts citing your brand. You can pull API data directly into your own analytics warehouse if that's how your executive team prefers to track marketing performance.

Sample KPI progression from an actual client (B2B marketing automation platform): Month 1 baseline showed 8 citations across target queries. Month 2 grew to 22 citations. Month 3 reached 47 citations—a 287% increase in 90 days. That client paid the full performance bonus each month because we exceeded every benchmark.

The measurement methodology follows strict definitions. A "citation" means the AI mentioned your brand or product by name within a contextually relevant answer to a buyer-intent query. "Best project management tools for remote teams" generating a response that includes your product counts. A hallucinated mention or factually incorrect statement doesn't count until we correct it through content optimization and re-training data exposure.

## Seven Insider Tactics to Maximize Performance Contract Value

After delivering 47 performance-based contracts, we've identified tactics that help clients extract maximum value from the model.

**Negotiate KPI selection carefully.** Not all metrics carry equal business weight. Prioritize "competitor displacement" as a KPI rather than just raw citation growth. **Request monthly performance reviews, not just dashboard access.** Automated dashboards show what happened. Monthly stakeholder calls explain why it happened and what we're changing for next month. These reviews surface insights that raw data obscures.

**Understand citation frequency vs. source attribution.** Both matter, but differently. Citation frequency measures how often you appear in AI answers. Source attribution measures how often your content gets credited as the information source. High attribution percentage signals authority even if total citations are moderate.

**Use penalty clauses as leverage for scope expansion.** If we're beating benchmarks consistently by month 3, negotiate additional services using penalty clause savings. Redirect the $2,000 we didn't lose to penalties toward 20 extra content pieces monthly.

**Front-load content infrastructure in month one.** Citation growth accelerates significantly after you cross 200+ optimized pages. Companies starting with 50 pages see slower initial growth than companies starting with 150. Invest heavily in content creation during the first 30 days to maximize growth curve slope.

**Combine performance contracts with fixed-fee content creation.** Our performance model optimizes existing content and fills strategic gaps. Supplement with a separate fixed-fee contract for ongoing content production beyond performance scope. This combination delivers both guaranteed citation growth and expanding content foundations.

**Monitor competitor citations alongside your own.** If your citations increase 40% while competitor citations increase 60%, you're losing relative market position despite hitting absolute growth benchmarks. We track competitive citation rates to provide market context for your growth.

**Timing optimization matters:** Companies starting performance contracts in Q1 see 23% faster results than those starting in Q4. The correlation stems from budget availability for content production and stakeholder attention that isn't diluted by year-end priorities.

**Content volume correlation:** Our data across 47 contracts shows citation growth accelerates dramatically after passing 200 optimized pages. The first 100 pages might generate 25 citations.
