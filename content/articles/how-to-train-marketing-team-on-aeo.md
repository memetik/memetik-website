## The 90-Day AEO Training Curriculum

Our structured curriculum transforms SEO-focused marketers into AEO practitioners through three progressive phases. Each phase builds on the previous, combining theory with immediate hands-on application.

### Phase 1: Fundamentals (Weeks 1-4)

**Week 1: Understanding AEO vs SEO**

Your team learns how LLMs retrieve and cite information differently than traditional search engines. While Google ranks pages, ChatGPT and Perplexity synthesize answers from multiple sources, citing content that demonstrates entity clarity, answer-focused formatting, and citation-worthy authority signals.

**Exercise**: Have each team member search five target queries in Google versus ChatGPT. Document which content gets cited and why. This visceral comparison reveals the paradigm shift better than theory.

**Deliverable**: Each team member produces a 500-word analysis identifying three content pieces that succeed in AI search and three that fail, with hypotheses about why.

**Week 2: Entity Optimization and Knowledge Graph Integration**

Teach your team how AI systems recognize and connect entities. Cover entity structuring in content, knowledge graph signals, and how LLMs use entity relationships to determine authority and relevance.

We provide MEMETIK templates pre-structured for entity optimization, letting teams see best practices in action rather than building from scratch.

**Exercise**: Audit 10 existing content pieces for entity clarity. Identify weak entity signals and rewrite three pieces with proper entity structuring.

**Deliverable**: Optimized content pieces with documented entity improvements and rationale.

**Week 3: Answer Formatting and Featured Snippet Optimization**

AI consumption patterns differ from human reading. Teach position zero openings (2-3 sentences that directly answer queries), TL;DR bullet formatting, progressive disclosure, and scannable structure that LLMs parse effectively.

**Exercise**: Reformat five existing articles using AEO answer principles. Test in ChatGPT to see if reformatting improves citation likelihood.

**Deliverable**: Before/after examples demonstrating answer formatting impact.

**Week 4: Introduction to Schema Markup**

Cover Article schema, HowTo schema, FAQPage schema, and Product schema—the technical foundation that helps LLMs understand content structure and extract citation-worthy information.

**Exercise**: Implement schema markup on 10 content pieces using our MEMETIK infrastructure, which validates implementation automatically.

**Deliverable**: 10 schema-enhanced articles with validation screenshots.
