## Getting Started with AI Visibility Tracking

The good news: You can start measuring your AI visibility today, even before selecting a platform. The better news: Most SaaS companies discover they have enormous headroom for improvement, making the ROI case for dedicated AEO platforms straightforward.

**How to audit your current AI visibility (free manual method):**

1. **Create a query list:** Document 20-30 questions your ideal customers actually ask. Examples: "best [category] for [use case]," "how to choose [product type]," "[problem] solutions for [industry]," "compare [competitor A] vs [competitor B]"

2. **Test across multiple LLMs:** Enter each query in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini. Copy the full responses into a spreadsheet.

3. **Track citation patterns:** Note which brands appear in responses, position (top recommendation vs. mentioned in passing), and frequency. Calculate your "AI visibility score" as (queries where you're mentioned / total queries tested).

4. **Benchmark against competitors:** Most SaaS companies discover 2-3 competitors consistently appearing in AI citations while they're completely absent. This competitive intelligence alone justifies the audit effort.

5. **Identify content gaps:** Look for query patterns where you should appear but don't. These represent your highest-impact content opportunities.
