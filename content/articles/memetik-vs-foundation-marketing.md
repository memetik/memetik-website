## Pricing Comparison: Outcome-Based vs Time-Based Models

Pricing structures reveal everything about agency risk tolerance and client alignment. The difference between our approach and traditional agencies like Foundation Marketing isn't just about numbers—it's about who assumes the risk of your marketing investment.

**Traditional agency pricing operates on time-based models:**
- Monthly retainers ranging from $15,000-$30,000+ for comprehensive SEO
- Hourly billing for strategy, execution, and reporting
- 6-12 month minimum commitments required upfront
- Scope creep leading to additional charges beyond base retainer
- Payment required regardless of business outcomes

**This creates a fundamental misalignment.** The agency profits from hours worked, not results delivered. When campaigns underperform, they recommend "doubling down" with more budget. When results exceed expectations, you're still paying the same retainer. The financial incentive structure rewards effort, not outcomes.

**We built our pricing model to eliminate this misalignment entirely.** Our outcome-based pricing ties our compensation directly to measurable business results:

- Flexible contracts that adjust based on results achieved
- No hourly billing—you pay for outcomes, not timesheets
- Our success mathematically requires your success

**For budget-conscious founders, the risk calculation is straightforward.** A traditional agency charging $20,000/month with a 12-month contract requires $240,000 upfront with zero performance guarantees. If results don't materialize after six months, you've spent $120,000 with nothing to show for it—and you're still contractually obligated for six more months.

With our guaranteed performance model, you know exactly what outcomes to expect within 90 days. If we don't deliver, we don't get paid. This shifts the burden of proof from "did we do the work?" to "did we move your business forward?"

We include all of this in our outcome-based pricing because we're not billing for effort—we're accountable for results. The tools we use, the strategy we develop, the senior expertise we apply—these are investments we make to guarantee your success, not line items to inflate invoices.
