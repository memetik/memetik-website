## Your AEO Implementation Roadmap

Taking action on AI visibility starts today, not next quarter. Here's your 90-day implementation plan.

**Week 1: Baseline Your Current AI Visibility**

Start by manually testing 20-30 product and category queries in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. Ask the questions your customers ask: "best [your category] for [use case]," "how to choose [your product type]," "[your category] comparison."

Document everything. Are you mentioned? In what context—as a leader, an alternative, or not at all? Which competitors appear? How many competitors does the AI cite per response?

Calculate your current citation rate: the percentage of relevant queries where you're mentioned. If you appear in 4 out of 20 queries, your citation rate is 20%. This baseline becomes your benchmark for measuring improvement.

Identify your visibility gaps—query categories where competitors dominate but you're invisible. These represent your highest-opportunity optimization targets.

**Week 2-4: Audit Your Content for AEO Readiness**

Review existing content for direct-answer formatting. AI platforms favor content that clearly answers questions in the first paragraph, uses structured formatting (lists, headers, tables), and provides specific data points rather than marketing fluff.

Identify pages with E-E-A-T signals: author credentials, citations to authoritative sources, original data and research, detailed explanations demonstrating expertise. These signals increase citation likelihood.

Check structured data implementation. FAQ schema, Article schema, and HowTo schema help LLMs understand content structure and extract citation-worthy information.

Map content gaps where competitors get cited but you don't. If rivals appear for "enterprise CRM implementation challenges" but you don't have content addressing that query, you've found a gap.

**Month 2: Implement Tracking and Optimization**

Select your AEO tracking tool using the decision framework from the previous section. Set up competitive benchmarking for 5-10 key competitors so you measure relative performance, not just absolute metrics.

Begin systematic content optimization based on citation gaps identified in your audit. Prioritize high-value queries where you're currently invisible but competitors appear.
