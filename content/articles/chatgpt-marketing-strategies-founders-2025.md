### Strategy #1: Implement Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Before Your Competitors

AEO is fundamentally different from SEO. While SEO optimizes for search engine rankings, AEO optimizes for being cited by large language models. When someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best [your solution category]?", AEO determines whether you get mentioned.

LLMs select content to cite based on specific signals: authoritative domains, structured data markup, direct answer formatting, citation-worthy statistics, and recent publication dates. They prioritize content that provides clear, factual answers over promotional material.

The formatting matters as much as the substance. LLMs favor bullet lists, numbered steps, comparison tables, and content with clear subheadings. They cite sources that make their job easierâ€”giving them ready-to-use answers they can confidently share.

**Action step:** Audit your top 10 pages for "answer-worthy" formatting. Does each page directly answer a specific question? Is the answer structured for easy extraction? Do you have supporting data that makes the content citation-worthy?

AEO-optimized content gets cited 40% more frequently than traditional SEO content because it's engineered for how LLMs actually work, not how they're assumed to work.

### Strategy #4: Optimize for Structured Data (Article + FAQPage Schemas)

LLMs love structured data because it's easier to parse, validate, and cite with confidence. Content with FAQPage schema gets cited 58% more often in LLM responses because it provides exactly what AI assistants need: clear questions with authoritative answers.

Article schema signals that your content is published journalism, not promotional material. FAQPage schema gives LLMs ready-to-use Q&A pairs. HowTo schema provides step-by-step instructions. Product schema offers specifications and reviews.

Implementation doesn't require a development team. Schema markup generators are freely available, and most modern CMS platforms have plugins that handle technical implementation. The strategic decision is which schemas to prioritize on which pages.

Your homepage should have Organization schema. Product pages need Product schema. How-to content requires HowTo schema. Any page answering common questions benefits from FAQPage schema.

**Action step:** Add FAQPage schema to your five highest-traffic pages this week. Include 5-8 questions per page with concise, factual answers. This single implementation often generates measurable citation increases within 30 days.

### Strategy #5: Create Quotable, Statistical Content Assets

LLMs prioritize content with specific data, statistics, and research over generic advice. Content with original statistics receives 4.2x more backlinks and 3x more LLM citations because numbers are inherently citation-worthy.

You don't need a six-figure research budget. Customer surveys, usage data analysis, industry benchmarks from your unique perspective, and aggregated anonymized client results all qualify as original research.

The template is simple: "We surveyed 500 [your ICP] about [specific pain point] and found that [surprising statistic]." This creates a referenceable data point that LLMs cite when answering related questions.

Statistical content serves double duty: immediate citation value and long-tail SEO performance. Every industry publication that references your statistic becomes a backlink, which further signals authority to AI systems.

**Action step:** This month, conduct one simple customer survey asking 3-5 questions about their biggest challenges, current solutions, and decision criteria. Publish the results with clear statistics and promote it. Watch for citations within 60 days.
