# Intercom Strategy Content Drafts

## Source Angle
- Primary hook: Intercom has 41,000+ sites linking to it and real brand authority — but when a buyer asks ChatGPT or Gemini "best AI chatbot platform," Intercom almost never shows up. Zendesk gets 60× the organic traffic. The gap is not product. It is content.
- Why it matters now: AI answer surfaces are consolidating around whoever publishes decision-stage content first. No one in the customer messaging category — including Zendesk — has locked those surfaces yet. The window is open, but it is closing.
- Proof points to use: 34.8M monthly searches in the category, Intercom captures under 0.3% (92,712 visits). 6% ChatGPT mention rate (1 of 16 prompts), 0% on Gemini and Google AI Overviews. Zendesk at 5.6M organic visits from 818K keywords. 90% of category demand is unbranded — no one owns it yet. Modeled base case: 1M+ visits in 12 months from a focused buyer-guide strategy.
- Caveats / claims to avoid: Do not state revenue impact — revenue planning needs Intercom's ACV and funnel inputs. Traffic projections are modeled estimates, not guarantees. Perplexity data was unavailable, so do not claim full AI-platform coverage. Do not overstate Zendesk's weakness — they have massive content infrastructure, they just have not locked AI answers yet.
- Suggested CTA: Soft comment-based — "If you are running marketing at a SaaS company with strong brand authority but weak AI visibility, drop a comment or DM. Happy to share what we are seeing in the data."

## LinkedIn Post

Intercom has 41,000+ domains linking to it and one of the most recognized brands in customer messaging.

But when a buyer asks ChatGPT or Gemini "best AI chatbot platform," Intercom almost never shows up.

We mapped the category. 34.8 million monthly searches across AI chatbots, customer messaging, CRM, and helpdesk software. Intercom captures less than 0.3% of that traffic today. Zendesk gets 60× more organic visits.

The gap is not brand strength. It is content infrastructure. Zendesk has built the buyer guides, comparison pages, and evaluation content that search engines and AI models pull from. Intercom has not.

And here is what makes this urgent: when we tested 16 prompts across ChatGPT and Gemini, nobody in this category has locked AI answers yet. Zero prompt hits for Zendesk. Zero for Freshworks. The window to become the default recommendation is open right now.

Intercom's authority base — 41,000+ referring domains, over a million backlinks — means it could move faster than anyone else in the space. The modeled estimate is over 1M monthly visits within 12 months from a focused buyer-guide strategy alone. Revenue impact needs their actual ACV and funnel data to size, but the traffic opportunity is sitting there.

The pattern is the same across SaaS: strong brand, real authority, almost no presence on the surfaces where buyers are actually making decisions now.
