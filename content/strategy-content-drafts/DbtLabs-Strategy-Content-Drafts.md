### Body
We tested 16 real buyer prompts across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.

ChatGPT mentioned dbt zero times out of sixteen.

Gemini mentioned it once.

And here's the part that really stings — Coalesce, a company with one-tenth the backlink authority dbt has, is getting cited in AI answers for the exact queries dbt should dominate.

This isn't a brand problem. Everyone in data knows dbt.

It's a content infrastructure problem. dbt doesn't have the buyer guides, the comparison pages, the evaluation content that AI models need to confidently recommend a tool.

There are 11.7 million monthly searches in this category. Almost 98% of that demand is unbranded — people looking for solutions, not specific vendors.

And right now, that demand is flowing to competitors who built the pages AI can extract from — even when those competitors have weaker products.

The fix isn't complicated. It starts with decision-stage pages — buyer guides, head-to-head comparisons, evaluation frameworks — paired with off-site authority so AI systems actually trust the content.

Modeled out, that's north of 400,000 monthly visits within 12 months. But the real win is becoming the default answer when a data engineering lead asks any AI tool what to use.
