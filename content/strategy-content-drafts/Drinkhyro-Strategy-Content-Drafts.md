- Why it matters now: Hyro already has enough baseline authority to make a focused push, but right now larger brands are shaping both search results and AI-driven product recommendations first. The bigger prize is not competitor brand interception alone — 92.6% of the mapped demand is non-competitor and unbranded.
- Proof points to use: Hyro currently has 1,004 organic visits and 362 ranking keywords against 8,464,050 validated category demand; sampled visibility was 0/8 in ChatGPT, 0/8 in Gemini, and 0/8 in Google AI Overviews; buyer-guide demand alone is 211,170, with modeled 12-month traffic potential of 23,229 from that cluster; overall modeled targets are 70,857 in 6 months and 191,505 in 12 months; Hyro already has 74 referring domains, which is enough to support a focused first move.

Do not claim Hyro can outscale LMNT or Liquid I.V. overnight; the case is for focused decision-stage capture first.
- Suggested CTA: Ask founders to comment if they want the exact first 5 buyer-guide pages Hyro should ship.
